Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone, regardless of age, can be an organ donor. However, medical suitability is determined at the time of donation.

Organs that can be donated include the heart, liver, kidneys, lungs, pancreas, and corneas.

After registering as a donor, your information will be available to recipients in need. When a match is found, the hospital and medical team will coordinate the donation process.
