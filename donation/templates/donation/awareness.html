Organ Donation Awareness

Organ donation is the process of donating organs to save or improve someone's life. Here are some key facts about organ donation:

  • One organ donor can save up to 8 lives.
  • Organs that can be donated include the heart, liver, kidneys, lungs, pancreas, and corneas.
  • Anyone, regardless of age or medical history, can become an organ donor.
  • Organ donation helps reduce waiting times for critical transplants.
